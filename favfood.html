Ingredients for Ofe Ora Soup

Steps

Preparation:

  1. First, Prepare the soup stock, wash and add the beef/sharki/kpomo into a different
  2. In a large pot, add your beef/shaki/kpomo into a different pot. Add your seasoning; cubes of choice, salt, add water and cook for 20 mins. Add the dry fish, fresh pepper snails, stockfish and ogiri. Leave to cook further in the stock for 10 minutes.
  3. Pour your red oil into the pot and bring to a boil. Scoop spoons full of the cocoyam paste into the boiling mixture and leave until it boils out and all signs of lumps are gone. stir from time to time to check that the mixture is smooth.
  4. Add the crayfish and ground uziza seeds 'piper guineese' also traditionally known as (mkpuru uziza) into the pot and leave to cook for 10 mins.
  5. Next, add the uziza leaves. Leave to simmer for 1 minute before adding the Oha leaves. Leave to simmer for another 1 minute then turn of the burner. Serve with your favourite swallow of choice.
Ingredients Quantity
Fresh Oha leaves (Shreded with Hands) 120 grams
Beef or Goat meat 1 1/2 Kilo
Stockfish 500grams
ice fish or dried fish 2-3 pieces depending on size
Ogiri 3 packs/ 2 tablespoons
Ground crayfish One-quarter of measuring cup
palm oil One-half of measuring cup
Chili Pepper desired amount or 2 tablespoons
Salt, 4 cubes of seasoning one and a half table spoon
Thickner of choice (cocoyam paste, grounded achi, Grounded ofo, cornstarch, oats, All-purpose flour, etc)
