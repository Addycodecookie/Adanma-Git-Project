History of Excellence

As Florida’s first law school, Stetson College of Law has been nationally recognized by US News & World Report, ranking at the top spots for over two decades in Trial Advocacy and Legal Writing Education, now both ranked #3.

Experiential Learning Opportunities

A practical, hands-on approach that allows students to participate in over 350 clinic and externship courses and award-winning advocacy programs.

Career-Ready Curriculum

Our student-centered philosophy transitions classroom achievements into personal and professional success with access to 60+ skills electives and six J.D. concentration certificates:

  • Advocacy
  • Business Law
  • Elder Law
  • Environmental Law
  • International Law
  • Social Justice Advocacy

The Juris Doctor degree is a cornerstone of the legal profession and is required to practice law in Florida and beyond. Stetson Law has prepared students to become highly skilled, ethical lawyers and leaders. Our graduates include prominent attorneys, judges, CEOs and advocates for positive change.
